We retain enquiry data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, including legal or regulatory requirements related to our consultancy services.

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, or request the deletion of your personal data. To exercise these rights, please contact us via the email address provided in your initial enquiry response.
